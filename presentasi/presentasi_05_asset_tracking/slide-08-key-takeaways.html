Key Takeaways

1

APAR Crisis

800+ findings terkait APAR (Fire Extinguisher) - expired, rusak, missing. Ini compliance risk serius yang memerlukan immediate audit & replacement program.

2

Predictive Maintenance Opportunity

Equipment dengan high-frequency findings (Pump, Motor) menunjukkan pattern failure. Shift dari reactive ke predictive maintenance bisa significantly reduce downtime.

3

Asset Health Monitoring

Tracking per equipment unit (contoh: Pump A-01, Pump A-02) enable targeted intervention vs blanket maintenance - lebih efisien, cost-effective, dan mengurangi over-maintenance.

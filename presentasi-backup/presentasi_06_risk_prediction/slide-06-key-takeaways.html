Key Takeaways

1

Predictive Scheduling Works

Model dapat forecast high-risk scenarios dengan 75%+ accuracy. Ini enable proactive (bukan reactive) resource allocation untuk HSE team.

2

Top 3 High-Risk Scenarios Identified

COAL_HANDLING + Shift Malam + Weekend = 78% high-risk probability. UNKNOWN zone + Senin = 72%. SUPPORT + Pagi + Senin = 68%. Actionable insights untuk patrol scheduling.

3

Location Matters Most

Feature importance analysis: Zona & Area contribute 55% to prediction - far more than time-based factors. WHERE matters more than WHEN.
